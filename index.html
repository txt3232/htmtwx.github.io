How made Termux with XFCE

⚠️ WARNINGS BEFORE YOU START

1. Requires Android 8+ (preferably Android 10+)

2. At least 3 GB of free space

3. Stable internet connection (WI-FI is better)

4. Charging 50%+ (energy-intensive process)

5. Patience
